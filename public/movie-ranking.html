The Best Anime Series According to Nemanja

Here are top 3 animes you must watch!

Hunter x Hunter

"Hunter x Hunter" follows Gon Freecss, a young boy who becomes a Hunter to find his estranged father. Throughout his journey, he makes friends, faces powerful foes, and uncovers deep secrets about the Hunter world. The series is renowned for its intricate storytelling, character development, and unique Nen combat system, exploring themes of friendship, ambition, and morality.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

"Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood" follows brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric, who use alchemy in a desperate attempt to bring their mother back to life. After a catastrophic failure, Edward loses an arm and a leg, while Alphonse loses his entire body, leaving his soul bound to a suit of armor.
To restore their bodies, they search for the Philosopher's Stone, facing moral dilemmas, dark secrets, and powerful enemies along the way. The series explores themes of sacrifice, redemption, and the consequences of ambition.

Hajjime no Ippo

"Hajime no Ippo" is a sports anime and manga that follows Ippo Makunouchi, a shy high school student who discovers a passion for boxing after being saved from bullies by a professional boxer. Inspired, Ippo trains rigorously to pursue his dream of becoming a champion. The series showcases his growth as a boxer, the challenges he faces in the ring, and his friendships with fellow boxers.
It combines intense boxing action with humor and character development, emphasizing themes of perseverance, determination, and personal growth.
